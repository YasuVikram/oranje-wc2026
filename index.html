The detail

The assumption. Each path assumes the team wins its group (the top-seed route). Finishing 2nd would drop them onto the other side of the draw вЂ” usually a tougher path (see below).

Opponents. The bracket slots are fixed by FIFA, so early opponents are fairly certain; deeper rounds assume the favourite advances, so the names get less certain вЂ” shown by the confidence tag. Round-of-32 slots marked "3rd-place team" are filled by a best-third qualifier once all groups finish.

The percentages are anchored to the Opta supercomputer's published figures вЂ” each team's chance to advance from the group and to win the tournament вЂ” with equal-strength steps assumed for the rounds in between. They show the shape of the climb, not a precise forecast.
